Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about my services

How long does it take to complete a website?

The timeline depends on the project scope and complexity. A basic website typically takes 1-2 weeks, while more complex projects may take 3-4 weeks. I'll provide a detailed timeline after discussing your specific requirements.

What information do you need to start my project?

To get started, I'll need details about your business, target audience, design preferences, content (text and images), and any specific features you want. I provide a detailed questionnaire to gather all necessary information.

Do you offer website maintenance services?

Yes, I offer ongoing maintenance packages that include regular updates, security checks, backups, and technical support. These can be customized based on your needs and budget.

Can you help with website hosting and domain registration?

Absolutely! I can assist with recommending hosting providers, setting up your hosting account, and registering your domain name. I also offer GitHub hosting for static websites as part of my services.

What if I need changes after the website is completed?

I offer free minor revisions within the first month after delivery. After that, you can either request edits through my website editing service or purchase a maintenance package for ongoing support.
